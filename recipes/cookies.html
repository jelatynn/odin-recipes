Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Photos will be added soon!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp Maldon salt
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp butter (salted or unsalted)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the brown sugar, granulated sugar, and salt until combined
  2. In a small saucepan over medium heat, melt the butter. Whisk gently and consistently, and keep cooking the butter as it foams. Remove the butter from the heat and foam begins to subside and the butter starts to turn brown/smell nutty
  3. Pour the browned butter into the sugar mixture and whisk until combined. Let cool until room temperature
  4. Whisk in the egg, followed by the baking soda and vanilla extract.
  5. Next, use a spatula or wooden spoon to fold in the flour until fully combined.
  6. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes before scooping dough into 12-16 balls on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake at 375 F (190 C) for 12-13 minutes
