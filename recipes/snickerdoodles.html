Snickerdoodle Cookies

Photos will be added soon!

Ingredients

  • 2 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp cream of tartar
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 and 1/2 cups + a seperate 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 large eggs

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt until combined
  2. In an electric mixer with the paddle attachment, combine butter and first 1 and 1/2 cups sugar on medium speed until fluffy
  3. Scrape down the sides of the mixer and add eggs. Mix until combined
  4. Scrape down the sides of the mixer and add the dry ingredients. Mix until combined
  5. Refrigerate the dough for at least 1 hour
  6. In a small bowl, mix the extra 1/4 cup of sugar and 2 tbsp cinnamon until combined
  7. Use a small ice cream scoop to form balls of dough and roll each in the cinnamon sugar before placing on a baking sheet about 2 inches apart
  8. Bake at 400 F (about 205 C) for 10 minutes, rotating halfway through
