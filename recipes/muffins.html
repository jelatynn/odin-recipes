Banana Muffins

Photos will be added soon!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 3 large bananas (ideally overripe)

Steps

  1. Beat the egg with a whisk until the yolk and white are combined
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the sugar, flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt until combined
  3. Whisk the egg and oil into the dry ingredients
  4. Pour the batter into 12 muffin cups, leaving each 3/4 full
  5. Bake at 375 F (190 C) for 20 minutes
